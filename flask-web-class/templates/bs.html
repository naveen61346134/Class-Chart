Beam Search

Beam Search is a search algorithm widely utilized in artificial intelligence models, particularly in natural language processing and machine translation tasks. This heuristic search method efficiently explores the space of possible solutions during decoding processes, such as generating sequences of words. Beam Search maintains a set of the most promising candidates, or "beams," at each step, gradually narrowing down the possibilities. This approach balances exploration and exploitation, aiding in the generation of more accurate and contextually relevant outputs. With its ability to optimize computational resources while producing high-quality results, Beam Search has become a cornerstone in refining the precision and coherence of AI-generated sequences, contributing to the success of various language-centric applications and models.
