Artificial Inteligense

Artificial Intelligence (AI) models are essentially computer programs designed to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. Machine learning relies on algorithms to enable systems to learn and make predictions or decisions based on data. Neural networks, inspired by the structure of the human brain, are a popular architecture in many AI models.
