Top-K Sampling

Top-k sampling is a popular algorithm employed in natural language processing and machine learning models for generating diverse and contextually relevant sequences, such as text. In this algorithm, instead of selecting a single token at each step, the top-k most probable tokens are identified based on their likelihood scores. This approach introduces an element of randomness and diversity, as one token is then randomly sampled from this reduced set of top candidates. Top-k sampling strikes a balance between deterministic generation and pure randomness, allowing the model to explore various plausible options while maintaining control over the output's quality and coherence. This technique is valuable in scenarios where generating diverse and contextually rich sequences is crucial, enhancing the flexibility and creativity of AI models in tasks like language generation and text completion.
